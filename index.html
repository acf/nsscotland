NSScotland.

Designed by Alan in Caledonia.

The fifth year of Scotland's first conference for Cocoa developers
will be held in October 2016.

The two day community conference runs on Saturday 22nd and Sunday 23rd.

My intention this year (like we did in 2011) is to run the conference as cheaply as possible. I'd prefer not to have to handle any cash at all and find sponsors to meet costs directly.

If you are interested in sponsoring something, please get in touch. DMs are open at @PossiblyAlan or @NSScotland or you can mail me at alancfrancis@nsscotland.com

By having no costs, I dont have to charge for tickets, and so will (again, like 2011) be able to request you make a charity donation instead to reserve your space.
