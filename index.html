NSScotland.

Designed by Apple in California.
Made in Scotland from Girders.

The third year of Scotland's first conference for Cocoa developers
will be held on October 25th and 26th 2014.

This year we our subjects include
Image Processing on IOS, CoreMIDI, testing at Facebook,
polymorphism in Swift, Indie survival tales and hacking on your AppleTV.
