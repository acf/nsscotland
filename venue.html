NSScotland: Conference Venue.

The conference will be held in the John McIntyre Conference Centre at Pollock Halls of Residence in Edinburgh. Pollock Halls is a 30 minute walk from Edinburgh Waverley Station in the city centre, and 30 minute cab ride from Edinburgh Airport. There are plenty of accommodation options close by, including the venue itself.
