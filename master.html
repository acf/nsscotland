NSScotland: The Speakers.

Dave Addey

For Your UIs Only

Apple's Human Interface Guidelines give an excellent overview of when and where to use many of the UIKit controls. However, it's not always clear-cut as to which UIKit approach you should use, or when one control is more appropriate than another. This talk will build on the fact that the Guidelines are exactly that - guidelines - and will help you decide when it's time to expand on the guidelines' recommendations to suit your UI's needs.

Neil Inglis

Making Friends with NSDocument and UIDocument

Neil will explain how to harness the power of NSDocument/UIDocument to make your life as a developer easier and make sure that your document based applications include all the document features that users expect. He'll take an in-depth look into undo support, autosave, versioning, supporting iCloud and how to utilise the lower level write APIs to create flexible, cross platform document formats.
